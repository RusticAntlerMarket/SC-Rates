Grade Definitions

Grade A

Premium Brown

Gathered same season as shed. Rich natural brown color. Solid, hard, premium quality.

Grade B

Weathered White

Gathered 1+ year after shed. Sun-bleached with some cracks. Still solid but slightly weathered.

Grade C

Aged & Rustic

Gathered 2+ years after shed. White, chalky, often naturally chewed. Softer texture with authentic rustic character.

Collective Eligibility

Participation in The Shed Collective is by invitation and approval only. Rates displayed on this page apply exclusively to approved Shed Collective members. Sellers and antlers must be located within 30 miles of Rustic Antler Market HQ in Dubois, ID to be eligible for the program. Rustic Antler Market reserves the right to approve, decline, or discontinue any consignment relationship at its sole discretion.

For more information or to inquire about participation, please call us Monday–Friday, 9:00am–4:30pm.

Market reference prices reflect a composite rate based on regional buyer and seller activity across the Western U.S. antler market. Rates are reviewed and updated regularly to reflect current market conditions.

SC payout rates are guaranteed per pound for approved Collective members. Variable rate sellers are subject to market-based adjustments with guaranteed floor pricing. Fixed rate sellers are locked at the rate active at time of inventory. All rates are per pound of antler weight.
