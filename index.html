Grade Definitions

Grade A

Premium Brown

Gathered same season as shed. Rich natural brown color. Solid, hard, premium quality.

Grade B

Weathered White

Gathered 1+ year after shed. Sun-bleached with some cracks. Still solid but slightly weathered.

Grade C

Aged & Rustic

Gathered 2+ years after shed. White, chalky, often naturally chewed. Softer texture with authentic rustic character.
